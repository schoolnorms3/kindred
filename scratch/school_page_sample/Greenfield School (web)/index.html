About

About the school

Greenfield International School is a CBSE-affiliated, co-educational day school in Sector 51, Gurugram, educating children from Nursery through Class 10 since 2004. Spread across a purpose-built 4.5-acre campus, the school blends a strong academic core with sport, the arts and technology, so that every child grows in confidence as well as competence. With a 1:18 student-teacher ratio, a safety-first environment and an experienced, caring faculty, Greenfield has become one of the most trusted names for families in South Gurugram looking for an all-round, future-ready education close to home.

A learning philosophy built around the whole child

At Greenfield we believe a great school does more than prepare children for examinations — it prepares them for life. Our philosophy rests on three commitments: academic depth, character and curiosity. From the earliest years we focus on genuine understanding rather than rote memorisation, encouraging students to ask questions, test ideas and learn from their mistakes in a supportive setting. This concept-first approach runs consistently from Nursery to Class 10, so that each stage builds naturally and confidently on the one before it.

A curriculum that balances rigour and joy

The school follows the CBSE curriculum and the NCERT framework, enriched with in-house programmes in reading, spoken English, computational thinking and life skills. Lessons are planned around clear learning outcomes, but they are delivered through stories, projects, experiments and discussion so that learning stays engaging. Continuous assessment helps teachers identify where a child needs support early, and regular, constructive feedback keeps both students and parents informed about progress in a meaningful and low-stress way.

The foundational years

In Nursery and Kindergarten, children learn primarily through play, movement, music and structured exploration. Bright, safe and thoughtfully resourced classrooms give our youngest learners room to develop language, motor skills, social confidence and early numeracy at their own pace. A gentle settling-in process and a low child-to-adult ratio ensure that every child feels secure, seen and happy from the very first day they walk through our gates.

The primary and middle years

As children move into the primary and middle school, the curriculum widens to include the sciences, mathematics, social studies, languages, computer science and the arts. Dedicated subject laboratories, a well-stocked library and modern digital tools support deeper, hands-on learning. Students take part in projects, presentations, debates and inter-house competitions that build collaboration, communication and leadership alongside subject knowledge — skills that matter long after the school years are over.

Preparing for the board years

In the senior grades the focus sharpens towards board readiness, with structured revision, regular practice tests, doubt-clearing sessions and individual mentoring. Our consistently strong board results reflect not last-minute coaching but a steady, well-paced academic culture maintained over many years. Career-awareness sessions and guidance also help students and parents make informed choices about subject streams and future pathways.

Technology and future-ready skills

Smart classrooms, a dedicated computer lab and a coding and robotics programme introduce children to technology as creators, not just consumers. From early spoken-English practice to age-appropriate digital literacy and AI awareness, we prepare students for a world that will continue to change rapidly — while keeping screen time purposeful and balanced with plenty of real-world, hands-on activity.

Beyond the classroom

We believe that sport, the performing arts and co-curricular activities are central — not optional — to a child's education. Greenfield offers football, basketball, athletics, skating, yoga, music, dance, art, theatre and clubs ranging from robotics to public speaking. Annual events, exhibitions, field trips and community-service initiatives give children real opportunities to discover their interests, build resilience and learn to work as a team.

A safe and caring environment

The wellbeing of every child is our first responsibility. The campus is fully CCTV-monitored, with controlled entry, trained support staff, a dedicated medical room and clear safety protocols. Our school transport is GPS-enabled and attended, and our pastoral system ensures that each child has caring adults who know them well. We place equal emphasis on emotional wellbeing, anti-bullying values and inclusive, respectful behaviour.

An experienced and committed faculty

Our teachers are the heart of the school. Qualified, regularly trained and bringing several years of classroom experience, they combine subject expertise with genuine warmth. A favourable 1:18 student-teacher ratio means children receive the attention they need, and ongoing professional development keeps our teaching current, evidence-based and child-centred.

A genuine partnership with parents

We see parents as partners in their child's growth. A dedicated parent-communication app keeps families updated on attendance, homework, assessments, fees and events, while regular parent-teacher meetings open a two-way conversation about each child's progress. Transparent processes and an approachable admission and front-office team make day-to-day school life simple and stress-free.

Location and connectivity

Situated in Sector 51, Greenfield is easily accessible from Sectors 50, 52, 56 and 57 and the surrounding residential areas of South Gurugram, with safe transport routes covering most neighbourhoods. The location gives families the convenience of a quality school close to home, reducing travel time for children and making participation in after-school activities far easier.

Holistic, stress-free assessment

We assess to support learning, not to label children. Alongside written examinations in the senior grades, we use projects, presentations, observation and continuous classroom assessment to build a complete picture of each child. Report cards are clear and constructive, focusing on strengths and specific next steps rather than rank alone. This balanced approach keeps anxiety low, helps children stay motivated and gives parents a genuine understanding of how their child is growing — academically, socially and personally.

An inclusive, values-led community

Greenfield is a warm, diverse and respectful community where every child belongs. We actively teach values such as honesty, empathy, responsibility and respect for others, and we celebrate the many backgrounds our students come from. Through assemblies, house activities, community service and everyday interactions, children learn to be kind, confident and considerate citizens. We work closely with families to support children who need a little extra help, so that no student is left behind and every child can flourish in their own way.

A warm welcome to new families

Whether you are exploring schools for the first time or moving your child mid-year, our admissions team is here to guide you at every step — from your first enquiry and campus visit through to a smooth, well-supported start. We warmly invite you to visit the campus, meet our teachers and see for yourself the calm, happy and purposeful environment in which your child will learn and grow.

Choosing a school is one of the most important decisions a parent makes, and it deserves more than a list of features. Below is an honest, detailed look at what makes Greenfield International School different — and why hundreds of families across Gurugram have chosen to trust us with their children's education. From the strength of our academics to the safety of our campus and the breadth of opportunity beyond the classroom, each point reflects a deliberate, long-term commitment rather than a passing trend. Read on to understand exactly what your child's days, and years, here would look like.

1 · A strong academic foundation that lasts

Academic strength at Greenfield is the result of a consistent, well-structured system rather than pressure. Our concept-first approach ensures children genuinely understand what they learn, building knowledge that lasts well beyond the next test. Teachers plan lessons around clear outcomes, use continuous assessment to spot gaps early and provide targeted support so that no child is left behind. The result is a steady record of strong board outcomes and, just as importantly, confident learners who enjoy the process of learning. We measure success not only by marks, but by curiosity, comprehension and the ability to apply knowledge to new and unfamiliar situations.

2 · Modern infrastructure designed for learning

Learning is shaped by environment, and our 4.5-acre campus is built for it. Air-conditioned, smart-enabled classrooms, dedicated science and computer laboratories, a spacious library, art and music rooms and well-maintained sports facilities give children the resources to explore every subject deeply. Thoughtful design — natural light, safe play areas, clean and hygienic facilities and age-appropriate furniture — makes the campus comfortable and stimulating, so that students genuinely look forward to coming to school each morning and feel a real sense of belonging.

3 · Activity-based, hands-on learning

Children remember what they do far better than what they are simply told. That is why our classrooms are active places — full of experiments, manipulatives, group projects, role-play, story-telling and discussion. In the early years especially, play is treated as serious learning, developing language, reasoning and social skills naturally. As children grow, activity-based methods evolve into investigation, research and real-world problem solving, keeping motivation high and making abstract ideas concrete and memorable.

4 · A safe and secure campus

Nothing matters more to parents than knowing their child is safe, and we take that responsibility seriously. The campus is fully monitored by CCTV, with controlled access, trained and verified staff, visitor protocols and a dedicated medical room with a qualified attendant. Our buses are GPS-enabled, speed-governed and accompanied by trained attendants, and pick-up and drop-off are carefully supervised. Equally, we work hard on emotional safety — a kind, inclusive culture where children feel respected, heard and free to be themselves.

5 · Experienced, caring teachers

A school is only as good as its teachers, and ours are exceptional. Qualified and experienced, they are chosen as much for their warmth and patience as for their subject mastery. Regular professional development keeps their methods current and research-informed, while our favourable 1:18 student-teacher ratio ensures each child is known as an individual. Teachers track progress closely, celebrate effort, and reach out early when a child needs extra help — building the trusting relationships that make real learning possible.

6 · Sports and co-curricular exposure

We want every child to find something they love beyond the textbook. Our programme spans football, basketball, athletics, skating and yoga alongside music, dance, art, theatre, debate, robotics and a range of clubs. Inter-house competitions, annual events, exhibitions and field trips give students real stages on which to perform, compete and collaborate. These experiences build confidence, discipline, teamwork and resilience — qualities that shape character and stay with children for the rest of their lives.

7 · A transparent parent-communication system

Parents should never feel out of the loop, so we have invested in clear, consistent communication. A dedicated parent app shares attendance, homework, assessment results, fee details, circulars and event updates in real time. Regular parent-teacher meetings, open houses and an approachable front office mean concerns are heard and addressed quickly. We believe a strong home-school partnership is one of the biggest predictors of a child's success, and we work to make that partnership easy and genuine.

8 · A future-ready learning environment

The world our children will enter is changing fast, and we prepare them for it. Coding, robotics, AI awareness, spoken-English fluency, financial and digital literacy and life skills are woven into school life from the early grades. Just as importantly, we nurture the human skills technology cannot replace — critical thinking, creativity, empathy, communication and adaptability. Our aim is for every Greenfield student to leave not only with strong results, but with the confidence and capability to thrive in whatever comes next.

9 · A nurturing approach to the early years

The first years of school shape a child's lifelong attitude to learning, so we treat them with great care. Our early-years team is trained specifically in foundational education, using play, stories, music and structured activity to build language, curiosity and social confidence. A gentle, well-planned settling-in routine helps even the most hesitant child feel at home, while a low child-to-adult ratio means little ones are always supervised, comforted and encouraged. Parents of our youngest learners consistently tell us how quickly their children begin to look forward to school.

10 · Holistic assessment, not just exams

We deliberately move beyond a marks-only view of success. Children are assessed through projects, presentations, classroom participation and continuous observation as well as formal tests, giving teachers and parents a fuller, fairer picture of each child's growth. Reports highlight strengths and clear, actionable next steps, and our feedback culture keeps stress low while keeping expectations high. The aim is steady, confident progress — children who understand deeply and enjoy learning, rather than children who simply memorise for the next examination.

11 · An inclusive, values-led community

A school's culture is felt the moment you walk in, and ours is warm, respectful and inclusive. We welcome children from many backgrounds and actively teach values such as honesty, empathy, responsibility and kindness. House activities, assemblies, celebrations and community-service projects help children build friendships, develop leadership and learn to care for others. Where a child needs extra academic or emotional support, our teachers and counsellors work closely with the family, so that every student feels they truly belong and can thrive.

12 · A proven track record of outcomes

Reputation is earned over time, and Greenfield's has been built across two decades. Year after year our students achieve strong board results, gain admission to good senior schools and colleges, and win recognition in academics, sport, the arts and Olympiads. More telling than any single statistic is the loyalty of our families — siblings who follow older brothers and sisters, and parents who recommend us to their friends. That trust is the clearest evidence that what we do, we do consistently and well.

13 · A convenient location and safe transport

A quality education should not come with an exhausting commute. Located in Sector 51, Greenfield is easily reached from across South Gurugram, and our GPS-enabled, attended bus fleet covers most surrounding sectors on safe, well-planned routes. Shorter travel times mean children arrive fresh and leave with energy to spare for activities, homework and family. For working parents in particular, dependable, transparent transport with live updates removes one of the biggest day-to-day worries of school life.

14 · Genuine long-term value

When you choose a school you are investing in years of your child's life, and we work hard to make that investment worthwhile. Our fees reflect modern facilities, qualified teachers, small classes and a rich programme of academics, sport and the arts — with no hidden surprises and clear, upfront information. Just as importantly, the confidence, skills and friendships your child builds here last far beyond the school gates. We believe value is measured not only in rupees, but in the kind of person your child becomes.

What this means for your child

Taken together, these strengths add up to something simple: a school where your child is safe, known and genuinely supported, taught well by people who care, and given a wide canvas to discover who they are. We would be delighted to show you all of this in person. Schedule a visit, meet our team, walk the campus and ask us anything — and let us help you decide whether Greenfield is the right place for your child's next chapter.
